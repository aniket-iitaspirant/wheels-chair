WHO WE ARE

Izzy Wheels was founded by the wonderfully
colourful Irish sisters Ailbhe and Izzy. Izzy has
been a wheelchair user all of her life. Ailbhe
created a range of stylish wheel covers for her
sister's chair so that she could express her
personality. They now collaborate with artists
and fashion designers all over the world to
transform wheelchairs into fashion statements.
